About Us

Every kid should have the opportunity to learn how to read. Our app will give every kid the opportunity to read. This will empower every kid to achieve success later in life. We will provide every kid with the connection to the the books in a deeper way by connecting them to the author through an online platform. We specialize in parents of children who would like to teach their children to read. We will connect the author to the reader through our digital publishing service. This will connect authors ranging from those who just started writing to seasoned professionals. Reignite kids passion in reading and future growth in knowledge.
