About us:

Sullen Interactive is a horror focused indie video game developer based in Seattle, Washington that is developing and self publishing their first title, Scarry Nite. This game, along with all games Sullen Interactive plans to develop, will focus on six elements of horror. These elements being disempowerment, isolation, tension, uncertainty, lingering fear, and an imperfect protagonist. The elements that will remain secondary and tertiary are graphics and jump scares to elicit a surprise rather than fear.


Mission Statement:

To introduce gamers into a world of dread and unease, but also hope and compassion


Vision Statement:

Fair and equal treatment of employee and customer alike
